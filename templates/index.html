Key Information About Our Advertising Campaign

Monster Partner Advertising Campaign is seeking residents of the United States to participate in our exciting new initiative. We are launching a scalable, out-of-home campaign using wrapped rideshare, individual, and delivery vehicles to achieve citywide reach. Participating vehicles follow high- and low-mileage routes through neighborhoods, business districts, commuter paths, and major events, creating repeat visibility and broad brand awareness at street level.

How Our Program Works:

Payouts for our 'Paid to Drive' program start at $300 per month and increase based on campaign criteria and extra earning opportunities. While the average monthly payout for participants ranges between $300 and $600, top earners can make upwards of $1,000. We maintain a strict no-cost policy: we will never ask you to transfer funds to us or pay a wrap shop with your own money. We handle all expenses, ensuring absolutely zero financial risk to you. Additionally, we manage the entire installation process using only certified technicians to handle the high quality vinyl application and removal, ensuring your car’s factory paint remains protected throughout the campaign.

We seek individuals who can seamlessly integrate our vehicle advertisements into their existing daily routines. To measure the campaign's success, we utilize location services via your smartphone to monitor movement and track performance in your area. Your specific location data is always anonymized and is never shared with third parties.
